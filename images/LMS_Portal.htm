Leave Management System.

LMS enables employees to apply or cancel leaves, display full leaves reports or a summarised version

In addition, it enables promoters to either approve or decline their employees requests and to view their teams monthly leaves schedule.

Things To Know.

  1. When applying for leave:
    • The Full/Half Day, Start time and End time fields are applicable when the request is for 1 (one) day only.
    • The Start time and End time fields are auto-populated based on the selection in the Full/Half Day field.
    • When the request is for more than 1 (one) day, the Start time and End time fields get auto-populated with 00:00:00.
    • Click the submit button once and wait. The speed of the response from the server depends on the network connection quality. The better the connection, the faster the response from the server and the worst the connection, the slower the response from the server
  2. The data displayed under Leave Request and Leave Balance pages are updated/changed only after the request submitted has been approved.

Promoters

  • Whenever your employee submit a request, you will receive an email containing a link to either approve or decline the request.
  • You will not be able to edit the request without accessing it via the link.
  • The View Calendar page enables you to view your teams daily, weekly and monthly leave schedules.
  • Whenever a request is not attended to within 3 days of its initiation, the request will be escalated to your promoter.
